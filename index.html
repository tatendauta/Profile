How I Work

Analysts deliver the most value when they stop just reporting what happened and start helping decide what to do next.

1. Start with the Business Problem

I don't open a spreadsheet until I know what we're actually trying to answer. "Why is funding down?" gets you a different — and better — analysis than "pull the funding numbers."

2. Make Metrics Trustworthy

If people don't trust the data, nothing built on top of it matters. I clean up definitions, fix reporting lag, and make sure everyone's looking at the same source of truth.

3. Bring Stakeholders Along Early

Findings shouldn't be a surprise. I check assumptions with product, design, compliance, and engineering before I finalize anything.

4. Measure Outcomes, Not Outputs

Scripts written and dashboards shipped don't matter. What matters is the bottleneck removed, the hours saved, the decision made with confidence.
